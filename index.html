Quick Tips

Below are some tips on how to keep you safe on your bike and also tips for drivers in a car to keep bicyclist safe.

Wear a Helmet !

Wearing a helmet is very important watch our video on the difference between wearing a helmet or not.

Stay Visible

It is very important to stay visible while biking to reduce the chance of being hit, this means if it is dark have lights and reflective clothing on.

Weather

If it is raining the ground will be slick and your stopping distances change dramatically, also there is less visibility so treat this as night time and be well lit.

Be Alert !

Behind the wheel or handle bars it is important to be alert of your surroundings.

Distracted Driving

Your car is a phone free zone. There is no reason to be distracted by your phone while driving or riding a bike. Visit The Horn Law for more statistics on Distracted Driving.

Drive / Ride Sober

Driving or riding under the influence is extremely dangerous and should never be done call for a ride and don't drive!
